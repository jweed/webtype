About the Beauty Desk

Beauty Desk is an interactive collage tool, which abstracts the process of makeup application. Rather than using common cosmetic products, use the set of collage accessories within the tool kit on the left of the page to layer on your video feed. Consider the application of these elements not to reconstruct, but rather to underline, accentuate, and accessorize your existing features. You may also choose a frame, and input your own association with the word "beauty" on the right side of your screen. Enable your video to begin!
